# Codex Workflow Policy

## Inspect before editing

Inspect existing files, tests, validators, and governance before making changes.

## Preserve architecture

Do not invent paths, authorities, or subsystems. Use existing project structure.

## Avoid broad rewrites

Do not rewrite files wholesale unless explicitly requested.

## Dirty worktree discipline

Do not stage or revert unrelated dirty files. Use explicit paths for staging.

## Checks

Do not claim checks passed unless they were run. Report skipped checks and
reasons.

## Final report

Report changed files, created files, preserved areas, checks run, failures or
skips, and remaining risks.

## Advisory findings

Classify advisory findings. Do not hide them by weakening validators.
